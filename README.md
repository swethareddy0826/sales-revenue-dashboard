# Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive dashboard built using Power BI to analyze sales and revenue data. It helps in understanding key business metrics, trends, and performance across regions and categories.

---

## Key Features

* KPI tracking (Total Sales, Total Orders, Products, Categories)
* Sales trend analysis over time
* Region-wise and category-wise sales distribution
* Top-performing products identification
* Interactive filters (Region, Category)

---

## Key Insights

* West region generates the highest sales contribution
* Sales show consistent growth over the years
* Top 5 products contribute a major share of revenue
* Technology and Furniture categories dominate overall sales
